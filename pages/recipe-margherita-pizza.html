Italian Medium 40 mins

Margherita Pizza

The classic: bright tomato sauce, creamy mozzarella, and fresh basil on a crisp, chewy crust.

⏱ Prep 15m 🔥 Bake 12–15m 👤 Serves 2

Chef notes

Preheat your oven fully and use a hot baking stone or inverted sheet for best crust. Drain mozzarella lightly to avoid excess moisture.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base or dough
  • 150 ml tomato sauce
  • 200 g fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (or as hot as it goes with a stone inside).
  2. Spread tomato sauce over the base; season lightly.
  3. Arrange mozzarella slices; drizzle with olive oil.
  4. Bake 12–15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese bubbles.
  5. Top with fresh basil and a final drizzle of olive oil before serving.

Serving ideas

Serve with a simple arugula salad. Add chili flakes if you like heat.

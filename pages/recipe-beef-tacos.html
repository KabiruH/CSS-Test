Mexican Easy 25 mins

Beef Tacos

Seasoned ground beef tucked in warm tortillas with fresh salsa, cheese, and sour cream. Fast, flavorful, and perfect for weeknights.

⏱ Prep 10m 🔥 Cook 15m 👤 Serves 4

Chef notes

Toast spices briefly in the pan to bloom their flavor. Warm tortillas just before serving so they stay soft and pliable.

Ingredients

  • 500 g ground beef
  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Salt & black pepper
  • Salsa, cheese, sour cream to serve
  • Oil for sautéing

Method

  1. Sauté onion in a little oil over medium heat until soft.
  2. Add ground beef; cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Stir in cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper; cook 1–2 minutes.
  4. Warm tortillas in a dry pan or microwave.
  5. Assemble tacos with beef, salsa, cheese, and sour cream. Serve immediately.

Serving ideas

Add shredded lettuce, pickled onions, or a squeeze of lime for freshness. Swap beef for beans for a vegetarian take.

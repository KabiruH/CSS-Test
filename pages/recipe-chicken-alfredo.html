Pasta Comfort 30 mins

Creamy Chicken Alfredo

Tender chicken, silky fettuccine, and a rich parmesan cream sauce that comes together in one pan. Perfect for weeknights but worthy of guests.

⏱ Prep 10m 🔥 Cook 20m 👤 Serves 4

Chef notes

Reserve some pasta water to loosen the sauce. Use freshly grated parmesan for best texture, and finish with a squeeze of lemon if you like it brighter.

Ingredients

  • 300 g fettuccine
  • 2 chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 200 ml heavy cream
  • 50 g parmesan, freshly grated
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & black pepper, to taste
  • Optional: parsley & lemon wedge to serve

Method

  1. Cook fettuccine in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water, drain.
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Sear in olive oil over medium-high until golden; set aside.
  3. Lower heat, add butter and garlic; cook 30–45 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Pour in cream and simmer gently 2–3 minutes. Whisk in parmesan until smooth.
  5. Return chicken and pasta to the pan. Toss, adding splashes of pasta water until glossy.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning. Finish with parsley and a squeeze of lemon if desired.

Serving ideas

Pair with a simple green salad or roasted broccoli. Leftovers reheat best with a splash of water in a pan over low heat.

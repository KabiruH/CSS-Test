Indian Medium 45 mins

Butter Chicken

Tender chicken simmered in a rich, creamy tomato gravy scented with warm spices. Classic comfort, great with rice or naan.

⏱ Prep 15m 🔥 Cook 30m 👤 Serves 4

Chef notes

Marinate the chicken for deeper flavor. Finish with a knob of butter and a splash of cream for silkiness.

Ingredients

  • 600 g chicken breast, cubed
  • 400 ml tomato purée
  • 150 ml heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt & pepper

Method

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, turmeric, and cumin for 20 minutes (optional but recommended).
  2. Sear chicken in butter over medium heat until golden; set aside.
  3. In the same pan, cook garlic 1 minute, then add tomato purée; simmer 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in cream and garam masala. Return chicken; simmer 10 minutes more. Season to taste.
  5. Serve hot with rice or naan.

Serving ideas

Top with fresh cilantro. Add a pinch of chili for heat, or a spoon of sugar if you prefer it milder.

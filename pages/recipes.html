Chicken Alfredo

A creamy pasta dish made with tender chicken, fettuccine noodles, and a rich Alfredo sauce. Perfect for a comforting weeknight dinner.

⏱ 30 mins 👤 Serves 4 🍽 Easy

Ingredients

  • 300g fettuccine pasta
  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 50g parmesan cheese, grated
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook fettuccine according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Cook in a pan over medium heat until golden. Set aside.
  3. In the same pan, melt butter and sauté garlic for 1 minute.
  4. Pour in heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer. Stir in parmesan until smooth.
  5. Add pasta and chicken back into the pan. Toss to coat and serve immediately.

Beef Tacos

A flavourful Mexican dish made with seasoned ground beef, served in warm tortillas and topped with fresh salsa, cheese, and sour cream.

⏱ 25 mins 👤 Serves 4 🍽 Easy

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 onion, diced
  • Salsa, cheese and sour cream to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add onion and cook until soft.
  2. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Stir in cumin, chili powder, salt and pepper. Cook for 2 more minutes.
  4. Warm tortillas in a dry pan or microwave.
  5. Assemble tacos with beef, salsa, cheese and sour cream.

Butter Chicken

A popular Indian dish made with tender chicken cooked in a rich and creamy tomato-based sauce. Best served with rice or naan bread.

⏱ 45 mins 👤 Serves 4 🍽 Medium

Ingredients

  • 600g chicken breast, cubed
  • 400ml tomato puree
  • 150ml heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in yoghurt, turmeric and cumin for 20 minutes.
  2. Cook chicken in butter over medium heat until golden. Set aside.
  3. In the same pan, cook garlic for 1 minute then add tomato puree.
  4. Simmer for 10 minutes then stir in cream and garam masala.
  5. Return chicken to the pan and simmer for a further 10 minutes. Serve with rice.

Beef Chow Mein

A delicious Chinese stir-fry made with noodles, tender beef strips and vegetables tossed in a savoury sauce.

⏱ 30 mins 👤 Serves 4 🍽 Easy

Ingredients

  • 300g chow mein noodles
  • 400g beef strips
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 2 spring onions, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions

  1. Cook noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Stir-fry beef in a hot wok until browned. Set aside.
  3. In the same wok, stir-fry garlic, carrot and spring onions for 2 minutes.
  4. Add noodles, soy sauce and oyster sauce. Toss everything together.
  5. Return beef to the wok, mix well and serve hot.

Margherita Pizza

A classic Italian pizza topped with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil.

⏱ 40 mins 👤 Serves 2 🍽 Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base or dough
  • 150ml tomato sauce
  • 200g fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C.
  2. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the pizza base.
  3. Arrange mozzarella slices on top.
  4. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.
  5. Top with fresh basil and a drizzle of olive oil before serving.

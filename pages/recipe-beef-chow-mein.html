Stir-fry Easy 30 mins

Beef Chow Mein

Tender beef strips, vegetables, and noodles tossed in a savory sauce. A quick wok dinner with plenty of flavor.

⏱ Prep 10m 🔥 Cook 20m 👤 Serves 4

Chef notes

Have all ingredients prepped before cooking—stir-fries move fast. Finish with a splash of sesame oil off the heat for aroma.

Ingredients

  • 300 g chow mein noodles
  • 400 g beef strips
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 2 spring onions, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Oil for stir-fry

Method

  1. Cook noodles per package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. Stir-fry beef in hot oil until browned; set aside.
  3. In the same wok, stir-fry garlic, carrot, and spring onions for 2 minutes.
  4. Add noodles, soy sauce, and oyster sauce; toss to coat.
  5. Return beef, mix well, and serve hot.

Serving ideas

Top with toasted sesame seeds or chili crisp. Add bok choy or bell pepper for extra crunch.
